Billy's Top 3 Restaraunt Recommendations

Saipan is completely cluttered with restaraunts of utter mediocrity and uninspiring food. Here are my top recommendations for people who wish to get more out of the local cuisine.
(Changes frequently, depending on paid advertisements)

My Top 3:

  1. MARVZ Kitchen
  2. The Angry Penne
  3. Sushi Spot

MARVZ Kitchen | Chalan Kanoa, Saipan

MARVZ Kitchen has amazing filipino food. Not your run of the mill, typical dishes. Made masterfully by someone who absolutely loves what they do, which is cook.

What I love about MARVZ Kitchen:

  • Genuine Filipino Food
  • Quick and easy
  • Owned by my Dad (Not a paid advertisement)

he Angry Penne | Garapan, Saipan

The Angry Penne has the most genuine italian food you can have on the island. Incomparable to anywhere else.

  • Genuine Italian Food
  • Atmospheric
  • Premium Dining Experience

The Sushi Spot | Middle Road, Saipan

The Sushi Spot is a new and upcoming restaraunt that has already won the title for best sushi on the island.

  • Genuine Sushi
  • Genuine Sushi Chefs
  • A third thing, I'm a web dev not real food critic
